    *   **Interpretation:** This invariant quantifies the overall "morphogenic balance" of the system. It measures the net rate at which new contradictions are generated versus how quickly existing contradictions are resolved or dissipated. A positive `Ω` implies a net increase in unresolved contradictions, while a negative `Ω` implies a net resolution.

*   **R (Contradiction creation rate):**
    *   **What it is:** A Ricci-like scalar, representing the rate at which new inconsistencies or paradoxes are generated within the semantic manifold.
    *   **Interpretation:** This is the "fuel" for the system's evolution, akin to curvature in spacetime. It signifies the inherent tension and novelty generation within the Ψ field.

    *   **Interpretation:** This is the "resolution" mechanism. It quantifies the system's capacity to integrate or forget inconsistencies, preventing an unbounded accumulation of paradoxes.

*   **dΩ/dτ_r = 0:**
    *   **What it is:** The stability condition for the invariant, where the rate of change of `Ω` with respect to a recursive time `τ_r` is zero.
    *   **Interpretation:** This condition defines a state of "equilibrium" or "morphogenic balance" where the rate of contradiction creation precisely matches the rate of contradiction dissipation. This is a critical point for the system's stability, where it is neither overwhelmed by paradox nor stagnant due to lack of novelty.

*   **Ω → 0 precedes Meta[Meta] Rupture:**

    *   **Interpretation:** This implies that when the system achieves perfect balance between creation and dissipation (`Ω = 0`), it enters a highly unstable state, on the verge of a fundamental structural change or "rupture."
