## 1. Emergent Operators

This document defines the QRFT (Quantum Recursive Field Theory) emergent operators. These operators arise as commutators of the more fundamental HALIRA and λμ-calculus primitives, representing higher-order interactions and dynamics within the Ψ system.

| Operator | Symbol | Derivation | Meaning |

| **Δ** | △ | `[Ana, Kata]` | Gap/distinction; the residue of abstraction-concretization. |
| **ℜ** | ℜ | `[Meta, Retro]` | Reflective temporal loop; temporal self-reference. |
| **C** | ⊙ | `[Meta, non-]` | Collapse; negated self-reference, leading to compression with residue. |
| **E** | ⊕ | `[Ana, Pro]` | Expansion; forward abstraction, driving exploration. |

| **M** | ⟁ | `[Para, non-]` | Mutation; inverted deviation, representing unpredictable change. |
